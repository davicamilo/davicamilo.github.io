Research

We work at the Intersection of Engineering, Physics and Biology

The Physics of Aging

We investigate how yeast age from a Physics perspective. We look at physical properties like size, crowding and information

Brownian Motion and Anomalous Diffusion

We apply the Physics of Brownian Motion and Anomalous Diffusion to different phenomena

Microfluidics

We develop microfluidic devices to study yeast aging.

EC DNA

We are interested in understanding the role of Extrachromosomal DNA in aging.

Machine Learning

We use machine learning to predict lifespan of cells based on physical properties

Information and Decision Theory

We apply Information Theory and Decision Theory to understand cellular decisions.
