By This Time Tomorrow...

Photo: Tötös Ádám, https://unsplash.com/es/@toti1996

By This Time Tomorrow... is a pioneering analytics and simulation platform that applies the principles of physics—especially diffusion theory and complex systems modeling—to understand, forecast, and navigate financial markets. We harness tools like mean squared displacement (MSD), velocity autocorrelation, and anomalous diffusion to detect hidden patterns, regime shifts, and structural frictions in asset behavior. By blending data science, statistical mechanics, and financial intuition, we empower investors, researchers, and decision-makers with deeper insights into volatility, risk propagation, and emergent market dynamics. Whether you're exploring trends, modeling contagion, or decoding the noise, we bring the precision of physics to the unpredictability of markets.
